About the SU Flea Market

The Strathmore University Flea Market is more than just a marketplace — it’s a movement to foster innovation and community among students, staff, and vendors. Born from the idea of enabling students to showcase their creativity and business ideas, this platform gives every student the opportunity to become a digital entrepreneur.

Whether you’re buying a hand-knitted scarf, selling second-hand textbooks, or offering homemade cupcakes, the Flea Market connects people, ideas, and opportunity in one vibrant online ecosystem.

Our Mission

To create a secure, student-friendly digital space that promotes responsible entrepreneurship and supports creative local trade within the Strathmore University community.
